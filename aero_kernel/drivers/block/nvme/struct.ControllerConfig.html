fn set_enable(&mut self, enable: bool)

Sets the enable bit if enable is true, otherwise clears the enable bit.

When the enable bit is set, the shall process commands. On the other hand, when the bit is cleared, then the controller shall not process commands nor post completion queue entries to completion queues.
